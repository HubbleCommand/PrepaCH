# Prepa CH
An app to help better prepare for military service.

## Features
General app to help prep for ER / RS & CR & include general helpful information 

- Review (might be interesting to integrate with military learning services that already exists)
    - Ranks
	- NBC
	- San / Medic
	- Electrical reglements (& antenna distances from them)
	- Cleaning boots & gun
- Checklist for items needed (different for Ecole des Recrues & Cours de Repete, actually not they are basically the same)
- Check important dates
	- Obligatory shooting dates https://www.vtg.admin.ch/fr/mon-service-militaire/dates-de-convocation.html
	- Next Service / Repetition https://ssv-vva.esport.ch/p2plus/ssv/schiesstageabfragerec.asp
